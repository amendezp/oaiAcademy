Workflow

Turn scattered inputs into a campaign brief.

The goal is not to generate copy yet. The goal is to make sense of the campaign: audience, offer, product truths, message, tensions, and claims risk.

Inputs

What Codex should read

  • Messy launch notes and stakeholder chatter
  • Product truths, brand voice, and claims guardrails
  • Survey, review, and customer voice data
  • Channel asks, inventory, margin, and timing constraints
Outputs

What learners should get

  • A clear campaign summary and objective
  • Audience, insight, message ladder, and proof points
  • Open questions, tensions, and approval needs
  • Claims guardrails carried into the next workflow
Human review moment: Ask Codex to show what came from the files versus what it inferred. The brief should be useful, but it should not hide uncertainty.
