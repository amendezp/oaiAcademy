Turn the creative world into a production handoff

A creative direction becomes useful when every team knows what to make, how it should feel, which variants matter, and where review is required.

Inputs
  • The chosen creative world and rationale
  • Channel asks and deadline constraints
  • Brand voice, visual references, and claims guardrails
  • Audience insight and offer strategy
Outputs
  • Asset list and variant plan
  • Visual and copy direction by team
  • Review checkpoints and open approvals
  • Claims watch-outs carried into production

Step by step

  2. Step 2

    Copy prompt

    Creative World To Handoff

    Turn the chosen creative world into a production handoff for design, copy, paid media, lifecycle, and creator teams. Include the asset list, format and size guidance, visual direction, copy direction, required variants, review checkpoints, claims watch-outs, and open approvals.
  3. Step 3

    Check usefulness

    A handoff should answer what to make, why it matters, and what must be reviewed before launch.

Workflow

Translate creative direction into production instructions.

This is the bridge between strategy and execution: design, copy, paid media, lifecycle, and creators should all be able to work from the same direction.

Human review moment: Make sure the handoff is specific enough for action. If it reads like strategy language only, ask Codex to make it more operational.
