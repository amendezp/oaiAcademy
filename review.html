Step 5 of 5

Review the output before you share it

ChatGPT can accelerate the work, but Finance owns the numbers, assumptions, and final recommendation. Use this checklist before sharing any AI-assisted analysis.

Numbers

  • Totals tie to the source data.
  • Formulas are visible and auditable.
  • Units and rounding are consistent.
  • Placeholder metrics are clearly labeled.

Assumptions

  • Every external assumption has a source link.
  • As-of dates are visible.
  • Confidence levels are assigned.
  • Needs Review items are not hidden.

Recommendation

  • The headline is clear.
  • Risks are separated from actions.
  • Leadership decisions are explicit.
  • Finance judgment is applied before sharing.
