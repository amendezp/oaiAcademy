Workflow 2 of 5

Turn the brief into a visual mood board

Now that the strategy is clear, use Creative Production with the brief and provided images to create a visual direction mood board for the summer campaign.

Inputs
  • The campaign brief from Workflow 1
  • Provided brand and product reference images
  • Brand voice and visual style guidance
  • Claims guardrails and customer language
  • The audience insight, user value prop, and message ladder
Outputs
  • A visual direction mood board for the summer campaign
  • Visual mood, texture, photography, and color direction
  • Notes that connect the visuals back to the brief
  • Guidance on what to avoid so the work stays on brand
Using a plugin

We will use Creative Production for this workflow.

A plugin adds reusable workflows, skills, app connections, or other tools to Codex. In this step, call Creative Production with @Creative Production so Codex can turn the brief and images into a mood board.

You can review available plugins from the plugin tab before starting.

Step by step

Run the workflow

0 of 3 completed
  1. Step 1

    Review the brief

    Confirm the brief has a clear audience, insight, product story, and claims boundaries.

  2. Step 2

    Paste the prompt below

    Start by calling the Creative Production plugin with @, then ask for a visual direction mood board.

    Copy prompt

    Brief To Mood Board

    @Creative Production

Based on our brief and the images provided here, create a visual direction mood board for our summer campaign.

Show the recommended mood, visual language, color, texture, photography style, and a short rationale for why this direction fits Chill Nest.
  3. Step 3

    Answer clarifying questions if asked

    If Creative Production needs more inputs, it may open a panel with clarifying questions. If the brief and images are already clear, it may skip this and move straight into the mood board.

Workflow

Move from strategic brief to visual direction.

A useful mood board gives the team shared visual choices and constraints. It should make the campaign easier to recognize, review, and produce.

Human review moment: Pick the direction that best fits the brief. Codex can explore options quickly, but the team still decides what has the right tone and brand fit.
