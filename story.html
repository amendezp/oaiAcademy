Step 1 of 5

Find the finance story in the raw data

Start in ChatGPT. Upload the company profile, operating notes, country performance, regional performance, and historical statements. Ask for a story that separates growth, profitability, cash flow, risk, and open questions.

Inputs
  • ChillNest company profile
  • Operating notes from Finance, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Support
  • Country and monthly regional performance data
  • Historical P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow files
Outputs
  • A concise performance story with favorable and unfavorable drivers
  • Quality-of-growth callouts across revenue, margin, returns, and cash flow
  • Open verification items before the model is built
  • Three questions the DCF model should answer

Step by step

Run the workflow

0 of 4 completed
  1. Step 1

    Upload the files

    Open ChatGPT and upload the ChillNest profile, notes, regional data, country data, and historical statements.

  2. Step 2

    Paste the prompt below

    Use the copy button, then run the prompt in the same ChatGPT conversation.

    Copy prompt

    Tell the Performance Story

    Use the uploaded ChillNest files to tell the story behind performance.

Create a finance-ready readout with:
- what changed and why
- top favorable and unfavorable drivers by country or region
- quality of growth: revenue, margin, returns, ROAS, and free cash flow
- items that need human verification

Cite the source file or field for every material claim.

End with 3 questions the DCF model should answer.
  3. Step 3

    Check the evidence

    Look for file or field references next to material claims. Ask for citations if they are missing.

  4. Step 4

    Carry forward the questions

    Save the three DCF questions. They become the bridge into the spreadsheet model.

Workflow

Turn messy raw data into a clear finance story.

Finance teams are rarely asked to recite rows. They are asked what happened, why it matters, and what leaders should inspect next.

Human review moment: Ask yourself whether ChatGPT cited data fields for the claims it made. If not, ask it to revise with source references before moving on.
