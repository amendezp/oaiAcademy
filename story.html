Workflow 1 of 4

Find the finance story in the raw data

Use the company profile for business context, the country performance file for the numbers, and the operating notes for what likely explains the movement.

Inputs
  • Company profile PDF or TXT
  • Country performance CSV
  • Operating notes from Finance, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Support
  • Regional data and historical statements saved for Workflow 2
Outputs
  • A short finance brief with a headline and clear bullets
  • Favorable and unfavorable drivers by country or region
  • Quality-of-growth callouts across revenue, margin, returns, ROAS, and fulfillment signals
  • Open verification items, risks, and questions to validate

    Create a Short Finance Brief

    Use the uploaded ChillNest company profile, country performance CSV, and operating notes to create a finance brief under 150 words.

Include:
- a one-line headline
- what changed and why
- top favorable and unfavorable drivers by country or region
- quality of growth: revenue, margin, returns, ROAS, logistics, and stockouts
- 1-2 open questions, risks, or items that need human verification

Use the profile for business context, the CSV for numbers, and the notes for likely explanations. Cite the source file or field for material claims.
  3. Step 3

    Review the brief

    Check whether the story is coherent, source-backed, and useful enough for a finance audience before moving on.

Workflow

Turn messy raw data into a clear finance story.

Finance teams are rarely asked to recite rows. They are asked what happened, why it matters, and what leaders should inspect next.

Human review moment: Ask yourself whether the story makes sense and whether it is coherent with the data. Look for source fields next to numeric claims and notes next to explanations. ChatGPT can save a lot of time by creating the first draft, which is often the slowest part, but every output still needs review, subject matter judgment, and approval before it is used.
