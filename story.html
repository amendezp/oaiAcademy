Step 1 of 5

Find the finance story in the raw data

Start in ChatGPT. Upload the performance CSV and operating notes, then ask for a story that separates growth, profitability, risk, and open questions.

Why this matters

Finance teams are rarely asked to recite rows. They are asked what happened, why it matters, and what leaders should inspect next.

What good looks like

The answer should mention country-level drivers, distinguish profitable growth from revenue growth, and flag unsupported assumptions.

Copy prompt

Raw Data Story

Act as a finance analyst.

Use the uploaded ChillNest country performance data and operating notes.

Tell me the story behind the raw data. Do not just summarize rows.

Separate:
- revenue growth
- profitable growth
- operational risk
- country-level drivers
- items that require human verification before leadership sees the analysis

For each material claim, point to the source file or field that supports it.

End with 5 finance questions this analysis should answer in the model.
Human review moment: Ask yourself whether ChatGPT cited data fields for the claims it made. If not, ask it to revise with source references before moving on.
