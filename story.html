Find the finance story in the raw data

Start in ChatGPT. Upload the company profile, operating notes, country performance, regional performance, and historical statements. Ask for a story that separates growth, profitability, cash flow, risk, and open questions.

Why this matters

Finance teams are rarely asked to recite rows. They are asked what happened, why it matters, and what leaders should inspect next.

What good looks like

The answer should mention country-level drivers, distinguish profitable growth from revenue growth, and flag unsupported assumptions.

Copy prompt

Raw Data Story

Act as a finance analyst preparing a first-pass readout for a cross-functional Finance audience.

Use the uploaded ChillNest company profile, country performance data, monthly regional performance data, operating notes, historical P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow statement.

Goal: tell the business story behind the raw data and identify what the DCF should test. Do not build the model yet. Do not summarize row by row.

Produce:
1. Executive readout: 5 bullets max on what is happening, where it is happening, and why it matters.
2. Quality of growth: separate revenue growth, gross margin, contribution profit, ROAS, return rate, logistics cost, stockouts, and free cash flow conversion.
3. Regional and country drivers: rank the top 3 sources of upside and the top 3 sources of risk.
4. Cash and DCF signals: explain what the historical statements imply for working capital, capex, and free cash flow.
5. Data quality and verification log: list items that need human review before leadership sees the analysis.

Finance interpretation rules:
- Show country-level revenue, marketing spend, and contribution profit in $K.
- Show portfolio totals in $M, rounded to one decimal place.
- Use favorable / unfavorable language instead of positive / negative.
- Separate timing issues from recurring performance issues.
- Cite the source file and field for every material number or claim.

End with 5 model questions the DCF must answer, including forecast durability, free cash flow conversion, working capital, discount rate sensitivity, and which countries deserve more investment.
Human review moment: Ask yourself whether ChatGPT cited data fields for the claims it made. If not, ask it to revise with source references before moving on.
