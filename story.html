Workflow 1 of 5

Find the finance story in the raw data

Use the company profile for business context, the country performance file for the numbers, and the operating notes for what likely explains the movement.

Inputs
  • Company profile PDF or TXT
  • Country performance CSV
  • Operating notes from Finance, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Support
  • Regional data and historical statements saved for Workflow 2
Outputs
  • A concise performance story with favorable and unfavorable drivers
  • Quality-of-growth callouts across revenue, margin, contribution profit, returns, ROAS, and fulfillment signals
  • Open verification items, risks, and questions to validate
  • A short finance one-pager the audience can scan quickly

Step by step

Run the workflow

0 of 4 completed
  1. Step 1

    Upload three files

    Start with the company profile, country performance CSV, and operating notes. Keep the regional and historical files for the next workflow.

  2. Step 2

    Paste the prompt below

    Use the copy button, then run the prompt in the same ChatGPT conversation.

    Copy prompt

    Tell the Performance Story

    Use the uploaded ChillNest company profile, country performance file, and operating notes to tell me the story behind performance data.

Create a finance-ready readout with:
- what changed and why
- top favorable and unfavorable drivers by country or region
- quality of growth: revenue, margin, contribution profit, returns, ROAS, logistics, and stockouts
- items that need human verification

Use the company profile for what the business does and how it makes money. Use the country performance file for the numbers. Use the operating notes to explain likely drivers, risks, and business context.

The audience is a finance organization and decision makers across multiple finance roles and levels of seniority.

Cite the source file or field for every material claim.
  3. Step 3

    Check the evidence

    Look for field references next to numeric claims, profile references next to business context, and note references next to explanations.

  4. Step 4

    Create a one-page brief

    Turn the longer story into a short finance brief that leaders can scan quickly.

    Copy prompt

    Short Finance Brief

    Turn this performance story into a short finance one-pager brief.

Keep it under 150 words. Use:
- a one-line headline
- clear, organized bullet points
- 1-2 open questions or risks to validate

Make it concise enough for senior finance leaders and useful enough for finance partners across different roles.

Workflow

Turn messy raw data into a clear finance story.

Finance teams are rarely asked to recite rows. They are asked what happened, why it matters, and what leaders should inspect next.

Human review moment: Ask yourself whether ChatGPT cited data fields for the claims it made. If not, ask it to revise with source references before moving on.
